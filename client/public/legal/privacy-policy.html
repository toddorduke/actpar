Accountability Partner ("AP", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information: your name, email address, password, age, gender, city, and account type when you register
  • Profile content: profile picture, alter ego name, and any other details you add to your profile
  • Goals and progress: goals you set, daily check-ins, streaks, and progress updates
  • Posts and community content: posts, comments, and interactions you make in the Tribe Community and Pacts
  • Photos and videos: media files you choose to upload to the app
  • Connections: spark connection requests and accepted connections between users
  • Pact membership: pacts you create or join and your activity within them

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • Enable social features such as connections, community posts, and pacts
  • Track your goal progress and streaks
  • Send you notifications related to your account and activity (with your permission)
  • Respond to your support requests and communications
  • Comply with legal obligations

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: profile information, posts, and community activity you share within the app are visible to other users based on your privacy settings (Everyone, Tribe, or Connections only)
  • Service providers: we use Supabase to store and manage data securely on our behalf. They are bound by confidentiality obligations and may not use your data for any other purpose
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal process

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Photos and Videos

Photos and videos you upload are stored securely in our cloud storage. You control who can see your media through visibility settings (Everyone, Tribe, or Connections). You can delete your uploaded media at any time from within the app.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: you can view your account information and content within the app at any time
  • Update: you can edit your profile and account details in the Settings section
  • Delete: you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account. This action is irreversible.
  • Data portability: contact us to request a copy of your data
